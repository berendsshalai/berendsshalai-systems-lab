# Recruiter Brief

This project demonstrates business automation and data operations ability through working software, not only descriptions.

## What It Proves

- Process discovery: shift records and clock events are separated into clear inputs.
- Data modelling: records use stable identifiers and explicit timestamps.
- Automation: repeatable exception detection replaces manual checking patterns across roster quality, attendance events, and review queues.
- Privacy judgement: all samples are fictional and clean-room.
- Engineering discipline: tests, CLI, local MCP server, docs, CI, and publication gates are included.

## Why It Is Relevant

The same thinking applies to payroll evidence, reconciliation, workforce planning, recruitment routing, internal approvals, and operational dashboards.
