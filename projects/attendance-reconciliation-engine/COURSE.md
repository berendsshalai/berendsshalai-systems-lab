# Course Module: Attendance Reconciliation

## Learning Objectives

- Identify the difference between planned and actual attendance data.
- Define deterministic exception rules.
- Build an audit trail for human review.

## Exercise

Use the synthetic dataset to find absent-while-rostered, present-while-unrostered, late arrival, early departure and missing-clock-event cases.

## Quiz

1. Why should payroll rates stay in configuration?
2. What makes an exception auditable?
3. Why are synthetic fixtures important for publication?

## Answer Key

1. They vary by jurisdiction and organisation.
2. It includes source records, rule identifiers and review status.
3. They prove behaviour without exposing private data.
