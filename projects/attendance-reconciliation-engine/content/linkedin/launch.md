# LinkedIn Draft: Attendance Reconciliation Engine

I am building the Sha-Lai Berends Systems Lab as a clean-room open-source portfolio of practical administrative automation systems.

The first flagship project is an Attendance Reconciliation Engine: a local tool that compares planned shifts with attendance records and produces auditable exception queues.

The public version uses synthetic data, configurable rules and clear limitations. It is not payroll software and does not claim statutory compliance. The goal is to show how manual spreadsheet checks can become structured, testable and reusable systems without exposing private operational material.
